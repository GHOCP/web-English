It is ...

STRUCTUREMEANINGSENTENCE
It is worth noting that ... 值得注意的是 It's worth noting that the overall share of young adults living with their parents was not at a record high in 2014. 值得注意的是，与父母同住的年轻人比例在2014年并没有创下历史新高。
It's safe to say that 可以肯定地说 I think it is safe to say that very few students expend the effort to do quality work in school
It is my view that ... 我的观点是 It is my view that limiting the use of disposable plastic bags is of utmost significance.
It would be disingenuous of me to ... ... 是言不由衷的 It would be disingenuous of me to claim I had never seen it. 要说我从未看到过，那就是言不由衷了。
It is yet to ... 至今未... It has yet to be released in domestic cinemas in China.

In ~

STRUCTUREMEANINGSENTENCE
In this time of ... 在...时间里 -- In this time of fear 在这恐惧之时在这恐惧的时候在害怕的时候
-- In this time of training 在实训的这段日子
-- In this period of time 在这段时间这段时间里
In that/this sense, 从那/这种意义上说；在这层意义上 In that sense, the manufacturers have been the blind ones. 从这个意义上说，制造商是盲目的。
In that/this regard, 就 那/这 一 点/方面 而言，... We've made extraordinary progress as a society in that regard. 我们社会在那方面已经取得了巨大进步。
In crude terms, ... 简略地说 In crude terms, the causes of mental illness seem to be of three main kinds 简略地说，导致精神疾病看来有三个主要原因。

On ~

STRUCTUREMEANINGSENTENCE
One the downside, ... 不利的一面是，... On the downside, the data also reveals failures in public health that have caused surges in some illnesses.
On the upside, ... 从正面看；从好的方面来看 -- Inflation surprises on the upside. 通胀出乎意料地处于高位。
-- So on the upside: Childhood dreams realized!

With ~

STRUCTUREMEANINGSENTENCE
With all due respect, ... 恕我直言；冒昧地说 With all due respect, the figures simply do not support you on this.

What ~

STRUCTUREMEANINGSENTENCE
What deeply concerned me is that ... 让我相当忧心的是 ... What deeply concerned me is that it's almost impossible for him to recover and get back on court.
