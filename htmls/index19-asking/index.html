Time

STRUCTUREMEANINGSENTENCE
What time does ... ... 什么时候？ -- What time does the shuttle bus leave for the airport?
-- It leaves every half an hour. 每半小时有一班。
What's the time frame until ...? time frame: the length of time that is used or available for sth （用于某事的）一段时间 What's the time frame until it's received?
How long before ... 还要多久... -- How long before your journey? 还有多久才能开始你的旅程？
-- How long before you can get ready?
How long did it take from the time ... to the time... 从 ... 到 ... 一共用了多久 How long did it take from the time you placed your order to the time you received it
How much longer is this gonna take? 这还需要多久? How many days later ... How many days later did he wake up? 过了多少天他醒了?
